Known Probing Vectors — Baltic Domain

SOURCE: OPEN / FÖRSVARSMAKTEN
Vector Alpha — Gotland Approach

Kaliningrad departure, northeast track, probe toward Gotland sovereign airspace boundary. Primary Tu-22M3 + escort pattern. Tests QRA at F17 Blekinge / Ämari Estonia.

Vector Bravo — Finnish Gulf Transit

Pskov / Leningrad OB departure, westbound transit through Finnish Gulf international airspace. IL-20 ELINT pattern. Maps Finnish and Estonian air defense emissions.

Vector Charlie — High North Arc

Murmansk departure, arc across Norwegian Sea toward GIUK Gap. Tu-95 Bear long-range probing. Tests Norwegian and UK QRA. Periodic NATO intercept exercise.
